Bacalhau À Brás

Bacalhau À Brás(cod à la Brás), is a traditional Portuguese dish prepared with shredded, salted cod, onions, garlic, thin matchstick fried potatoes, and scrambled eggs, topped with black olives and sprinkled with fresh parsley.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 kg salted cod
  • 250g matchstick potato chips
  • 1 onion , finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic crushed
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 8 eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chopped parsley
  • Black Olives

Steps

  1. Soak the salted cod in water for 24 hours, changing the water 2 or 3 times.
  2. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  3. Lower the temperature and simmer the cod for about 10 minutes.
  4. Remove the cod pieces with a skimmer and onto a plate.
  5. Remove the skin from the cod and shred it.
  6. In a large skillet over medium heat, add the olive oil.
  7. Add the onion and garlic and sauté for 5 minutes while stirring.
  8. Add the cod, mix and continue to cook for 5 minutes.
  9. Add half of the matchstick chips and mix.
  10. In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  11. Add the eggs to the cod mixture and stir until the eggs are half-cooked.
  12. Add half of the matchstick chips, and stir one last time.
  13. Remove and arrange in a dish.
  14. Garnish with parsley and black olives.
